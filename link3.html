Udumalpet Nendhiran Banana Price Difference

The FarmDay mobile app, created for farmers, helps them understand accurate crop prices. Just months after its launch, it’s already revealing significant price differences across regions. For instance, on June 17, 2021, a farmer in Udumalpet, Tiruppur, discovered that *Nendran* bananas were sold at ₹30/kg in nearby Sathyamangalam on June 16, while he sold his for only ₹21/kg.
