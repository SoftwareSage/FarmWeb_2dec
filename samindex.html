About FarmDay


FarmDay mobile app is designed to empower farmers by delivering timely and accurate information. With the right insights at the right time, the app helps farmers boost their revenue or reduce input costs by leveraging advanced technology.

With this innovative Data driven digital ecosystem aimed at solving one problem at a time, we believe that it would eventually create a ripple ef ect by adding incremental value to farmers.

Download the App

Features

Real-Time Crop Prices

Brings real time prices of the crops at farmers fingertips empowering them to sell their crops at the best possible price.

Merchant Directory

Connect farmers with a wider network of buyers to increase their market reach and potential revenue.

Marketplace

Explore a comprehensive farming marketplace where you'll find everything from farming equipments, saplings and livestock to harvest.
