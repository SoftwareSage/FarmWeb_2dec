Sathy Mondhan Banana Price Difference

A FarmDay user from Sathyamangalam, Erode District, recently shared insights about the app. On July 24, 2021, he sold a bunch of *Monthan* bananas for ₹260, while another farmer just half a kilometer away sold similar quality bunches for ₹160.
